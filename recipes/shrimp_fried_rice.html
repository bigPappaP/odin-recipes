Shrimp Fried Rice

This recipe is pretty good looking so we are going to try to make it

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 tsp vegetable oil
  • 1/2 lb shrimp, peeled
  • 1 red bell pepper sliced into thin strips & halved
  • 1/2 cup sugar snap peas cut diagonally
  • 3 green onions cut diagonally, whites & greens separated
  • 1 tsp finely chopped gingerroot
  • 3 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 5 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp packed brown sugar
  • 1.5 cups hot cooked white rice (without salt/butter)
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds, toasted
  1. Wash your damn hands
  2. Heat 10-inch nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Add 1 teaspoon oil, and heat until shimmering. Add shrimp; cook 2 minutes without moving. Add Sriracha sauce, and stir. Cook 1 minute longer; transfer shrimp with slotted spoon to small bowl, and cover with foil.
  3. Add remaining 1 teaspoon oil, the bell pepper, peas and whites of onions to skillet, and cook 2 to 3 minutes or until softened, stirring constantly.
  4. Add gingerroot and garlic; cook and stir about 30 seconds longer or until fragrant.
  5. Add soy sauce and brown sugar; cook 1 minute longer.
  6. Add rice; cook about 1 minute or until broken up and warmed.
  7. Return shrimp to pan; cook 1 minute to combine flavors. Stir in lime juice. Top with onion greens and toasted sesame seed.
